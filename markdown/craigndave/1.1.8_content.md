### How to easily turn a positive number into its two’s complement negative version
- Let’s convert the number +12 into -12.
- Write out the positive version of the number.
- Starting from the right-hand digit (the least significant bit), copy out each digit exactly as it appears up to and including the first 1 you come across.
- From the next digit onwards, swap every 1 for a 0 and swap every 0 for a 1.
