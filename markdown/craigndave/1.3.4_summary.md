### Lossless compression methods: Run-length encoding (RLE)
- Huffman coding is often used to compressed text-based data.
- Meanwhile, run-length encoding is typically used to compress bitmap images.

- 22 0 6 1 8 0 1 1 8 0 1 1 8 0 1 1 8 0 1 1  8 0 6 1 22 0
- These are known as frequency/data pairs.
- With just two colours, we can encode an image with a colour depth of 1 bit (0 = white, 1 = black).
