### Packet and packet structure
- When data is sent over long distances it is normally broken down into packets. Packets tend to be quite small.
- Packets are split into three main parts:
  - Packet header:
    - Destination IP address
    - Sender’s IP address
    - Packet number – e.g., 1 of 5
  - Payload:
    - The actual data being transmitted
  - Trailer:
    - Marks the end of the packet
