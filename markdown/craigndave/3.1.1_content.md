### What is the role of a CPU?
- You can think of the CPU a bit like the brain of a computer.
- It is central to all modern computer systems, including desktop PCs, laptops, consoles, tablets, smartphones and more.

### What is a microprocessor?
- As with many areas of computer science, processing involves different terms that mean very similar things.
- The CPU is often referred to as a:
  - Microprocessor
  - Processor
  - Chip multiprocessor
- Although arguments can be made for defining these terms in different ways, for simplicity, you should think of the terms CPU and microprocessor as being interchangeable.
- CPUs/microprocessors are typically installed as an integrated circuit on a single physical microchip.

### The role of the CPU
- The central processing unit (CPU):
  - takes in data and instructions;
  - processes them; and
  - produces output.
- The terms CPU and microprocessor essentially mean the same thing.
- A CPU/microprocessor is typically installed as an integrated circuit on a single microchip.
