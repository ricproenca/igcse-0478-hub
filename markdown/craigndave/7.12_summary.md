### Verification
- Verification ensures that data is not changed, either accidentally or on purpose, as it is being entered.
- Double entry verification is a process whereby important data has to be entered twice, sometimes by different people. The system then compares both entries to check they are the same before accepting the data.
- A screen check or visual check is a manual check performed by the user who is entering the data.
