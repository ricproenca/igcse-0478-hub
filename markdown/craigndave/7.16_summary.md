### Algorithmic thinking
- The approach we have taken here could be used to tackle a range of problems.
- Break the problem down.
- Think about what data is important/not important.
- Consider the relevant data structures.
- Tackle each part of the problem step by step.
- Algorithmic thinking takes practice.
- The more you do it, the better you will become.
